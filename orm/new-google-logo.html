NEW Google Logo: High-quality PNG Image with Transparent Background

by SEO Mofo on May 24th, 2010

in Reputation Management

This website is not endorsed by nor associated with Google in any way.

I was searching for a high-resolution copy of Google’s new logo and couldn’t find one, so I made my own. I’m posting it here for anyone else who needs it. Enjoy.

To all the large companies out there: when you refuse to publish high-quality copies of your logos, you’re not protecting your brand–all you’re doing is forcing people to recreate your logo in Photoshop. In the end, what have you really accomplished? Instead of your authentic company logo being used without your permission…you now have a shitty knockoff being used without your permission.

And on that note, dear readers, I present to you…the new Google logo! I have two versions here, for your viewing pleasure. The first version is the highest-resolution official Google logo that I could find. I found it in the Google blog post, The Google design, turned up a notch. The only real limitation of this image is that it has a white background instead of a transparent background.
